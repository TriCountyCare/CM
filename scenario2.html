Comprehensive Care Management Scenario 2

Last week you held a Life Plan meeting for an individual you serve. The individual, family, and providers were all in agreement with the goals and services discussed in the meeting. Since that time you have updated the plan in the medisked to reflect those discussions and sent the revised draft for acknowledgement. The community habilitation provider calls you after receiving the plan and requests changes. How do you respond?.

  • Politely explain that you won't be making any changes because they had already agreed to the plan at the time of the meeting.
  • Ask what sort of changes they wanted to make.
  • Explain to the provider that you will happily make any changes they need, and ask them to email you the changes that they require.
