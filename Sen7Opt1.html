Ensuring "access" to plans is actually "acces" to plans

Can they access email? Should it be hand delivered and reviewed to ensure comprehenion? Is mail OK? Do they need large print? Is the writing level within the comprehension ability of the reader? Is it provided in a language which they can understand? (braille included) Are your Life Plans being distributed to the individual/family in a timely and responsive manner? Are you following up with bute providers to ensure that they are sending their plans to the individual/family as well? While it is not the responsibility of a Care Manager to distribute provider plans, it is the responsibility of the Care Manager to advocate for the individuals they serve.
